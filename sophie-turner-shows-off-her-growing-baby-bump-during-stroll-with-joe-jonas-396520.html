Sophie Turner Shows Off Her Growing Baby Bump During Stroll With Joe Jonas

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Former Game of Thrones star Sophie Turner and her husband Joe Jonas haven’t officially confirmed that they are expecting their first child together. But, after the couple took a stroll in Los Angeles, it’s clear they are going to be parents soon because Turner’s growing baby bump was on full display.

The 24-year-old British actress was wearing a black fitted sweatshirt, black leggings, and Ugg logo slippers during her walk with Jonas, and there was no hiding her pregnant belly. The singer was wearing a red t-shirt, jeans, and a jacket. The couple held hands during their stroll and they both wore black face masks.

https://twitter.com/LeighSophiet/status/1260327659583819776

According to Us Weekly, it was confirmed in February that Turner and Jonas were expecting a baby. The actress has been concealing her bump by wearing baggy sweatshirts and sweaters or baby doll dresses, but an insider dished that Turner is “due in the middle of summer.”

The couple has been in quarantine together since the start of the COVID-19 lockdown, and Turner told Conan O’Brien last month that the quarantine hasn’t been an issue because she is an introvert.

“I’m a homebody. If I could stay at home all day I would, so this is great for me. I leave the house like once a day anyway to walk my dogs and then that’s it. Everything seems to be working out in my favor here. … It’s like prison for [Jonas], but it’s great for me,” said Turner.

The parents-to-be have also been documenting their quarantine life on Instagram, and Turner dished that Jonas is the kind of guy who wears jeans at home during the lockdown.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B_fOtBqhFQg/

“I saw a meme online [that said], ‘If you’re wearing denim pants at home, what does that say about you during quarantine? What does that say about you as a person? Are you a psychopath?’ And Joe does that! Joe wears denim trousers at home when no one can see him. I’m not wearing denim trousers!” she told O’Brien.

Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner recently celebrated their first wedding anniversary on May 1. The couple tied the knot in Las Vegas last year in a surprise ceremony after the Billboard Music Awards. A few weeks later, the couple had a big, second wedding in front of family and friends at Chateau de Tourreau in France.
