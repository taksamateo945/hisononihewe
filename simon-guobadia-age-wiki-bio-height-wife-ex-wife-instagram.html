Who Is Simon Guobadia? Wiki / Biography 

Born Simon Lyore Guobadia, Simon is a businessman, entrepreneur, and a film producer. He received his education from Loyola College Ibadan in Nigeria. In 1993, he graduated from the University of the District of Columbia with a BBA in accounting. 

Despite the fact that his parents’ whereabouts are unknown, it is understood that he grew up with his siblings. One of his siblings, Simon’s younger sister is named Mary Awo-Osagie, age 45. Aside from that, he enjoys enjoys philanthropic work, traveling, and golfing.

Mr. Guobadia grew up in Nigeria but later moved to Georgia, USA. From 2004 to 2006, he served as the Tax manager at Deloitte in Atlanta. Simon has developed SIMCOL Petroleum into a national company as a first-generation owner.

The Chief Executive Officer has replicated this knowledge base and discipline into other philanthropic and business activities as an entrepreneur with over 10-years experience in the business.
