American football strong safety Tony Jefferson plays for NFL club, Baltimore Ravens. He is the 2009 recipient of USA Today High School All-American and 2010 winner of CFN, TSN Freshman All-American and Big 12 co-Defensive Freshman of the Year.

Tony Jefferson Bio & Family

The player was born as Tony Lemar Jefferson Jr. on 27 January 1992 in San Diego, California to parents Tony: a boxer of the early 1990s and Adria Jefferson. He went to Eastlake High School in Chula Vista, California and then attended the University of Oklahoma. In 2010, Tony was a Freshman All-American by College Football News.

Tony stands at the height of 5 feet 11 inches (1.80 m) tall and weighs around 98 kg.
