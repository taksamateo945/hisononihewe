See RHONJ stars Teresa Giudice, Dolores Catania and Jennifer Aydins changing looks after plastic

February 2024 · 4 minute read

REAL Housewives Of New Jersey stars Teresa Giudice, Dolores Catania, and Jennifer Aydin have gone through some plastic surgery makeovers, and even even been quite open about it.

Some of their other housewives’ costars, have also confirmed changing some of their looks.

? Follow all our latest news & stories on Real Housewives.

Teresa Guidice

Teresa, arguably the most popular of the Jersey housewives, has had no problem showing off her looks on Instagram as she shares plenty of photos, and back in January 2020 she opened up changing up her looks.

“I believe it’s important to always stand strong and feel like your best, most confident self,” she previously said, revealing that she got a breast augmentation.

The RHONJ star, 48, explained she “was very nervous to re-do [her] breasts,” but was happy with how they ended up looking.

She continued: “I encourage anyone who doesn’t feel their best self to work on feeling better. Even if it is the smallest thing. If it is something that requires cosmetic surgery to feel better.”

Teresa has also admitted to having lip fillers.

The reality star’s 20-year-old daughter Gia also previously admitted to getting a nose job.

Dolores Catania

On a recent episode of RHONJ, Dolores revealed that she hadn’t told her boyfriend David that she changed a bit about herself by getting surgery.

The Bravo star, 50, admitted to getting liposuction and a tummy tuck after losing weight amid the coronavirus pandemic in 2020.

During the episode, Dolores said in a confessional interview: “Things are a little dicey between me and David because I got a tummy tuck, a liposuction, a butt lift a little bit.

“And I didn’t tell him until the night before I was leaving because David is not for elective surgery.”

Jennifer Aydin

Just like her two costars, Jennifer has also been quite open about her plastic surgery makeover – however, it was her own husband who changed her looks.

The 43-year-old once revealed that her husband and surgeon, Dr. Bill Aydin, performed a breast reduction, liposuction, and a breast lift on the star.

She told Page Six: “I was really big on top, meaning my breasts were large, and I hated them.

“They were like these big melons. They were mushy from breastfeeding five kids and it started off with having the breast reduction. So that’s what I did first.”

Jennifer, who said Teresa’s pics inspired her to change, added: “It was just breast reduction and lift with implant…”

Melissa Gorga

Teresa’s sister-in-law Melissa, 41, has also made sure to give herself an update, telling Glamour in 2019: “I had breast implants, and I’ve had a nose job.”

She told the outlet: “My nose was definitely from suddenly going from being a housewife to being on TV and in magazines. I was like, ‘I never noticed that. I think if I do that little tweak, it’ll make me feel better.’

“And I did it and was happy with my results. I mean, everybody’s different but for me that’s what it was.”

Margaret Josephs

Last year, the 53-year-old opened up on Instagram and revealed: “I had this facelift revision after [Season 10] for reunion.”

Margaret explained she had lifted eyelids, her cheeks, and even “revised” her neckline and jawline.

Jackie Goldschneider

The changes keep on coming as the 44-year-old told Domenick Nati on his YouTube show in 2020 that she has also gone under the knife, mainly for a tummy tuck and breast implants – as well as a lip filler and Botox.
