Charlie Sheen Approves of Rangers P Derek Holland's 'Wild Thing' Haircut | News, Scores, Highlights,

January 2024 · 2 minute read
via @dutchoven45

Charlie Sheen is a noted Cincinnati Reds fan, but when he saw Texas Rangers pitcher Derek Holland paying tribute to "Wild Thing," he just had to go visit the Rangers.

Sheen dropped in on the Rangers' clubhouse at Angels Stadium on Saturday after Holland held the Los Angeles Angels to just one run on three hits in eight innings. That's when the Texas left-hander got to show the actor his Wild Thing haircut:

Getting Ricky Vaughn's approval—via Moby Picture as well—made this haircut totally worth it. 

The best part is that, according to the Dallas Morning News, Holland says the haircut will be staying for the rest of the year. As for what led him to pay tribute to the Wild Thing, here's what he had to say:

It was one of my friends actually. They came up with it and we had actually talked about this a long time ago. He said, ‘When you come back, that’s what you need to come back with.’ And I was like, ‘Nah. I don’t know if I could do it.’ They finally talked me into it. Obviously a few drinks got into me and that’s why I did it. I definitely did the haircut to change it up. I got to enjoy myself. That’s what it’s all about.

It will be a sad day if/when the southpaw changes his hairstyle.
